====================================
MailMergeKit - Sample Email Template
====================================

This is an example of how to structure your Word document for mail merge.

Instructions:
1. Copy the content below into a new Word document
2. Set up mail merge with the sample-data.csv file
3. Insert merge fields where indicated
4. Use MailMergeKit to create Outlook drafts

====================================

Subject (to configure in MailMergeKit):
Domain «Domain» expires on «ExpiryDate»

====================================

Email Body:

Dear «FirstName» «LastName»,

This is a reminder that your domain «Domain» registered with «Company» will expire on «ExpiryDate».

To ensure uninterrupted service, please renew your domain before the expiration date.

What happens if you don't renew:
• Your website will go offline
• Email services will stop working
• You may lose your domain name

Renewal is simple:
1. Log into your account
2. Navigate to domain management
3. Click "Renew" next to «Domain»
4. Complete the payment

If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Best regards,
The «Company» Support Team

====================================

Tips for Your Own Templates:

1. Use merge fields with the «FieldName» format
2. Keep your message clear and concise
3. Test with a small sample first (2-3 recipients)
4. Review drafts in Outlook before sending
5. Personalize subject lines for better open rates

====================================

Common Merge Fields:

«FirstName» - Recipient's first name
«LastName» - Recipient's last name
«Email» - Recipient's email address
«Company» - Company name
«Domain» - Domain name
«ExpiryDate» - Expiration date

You can create any field names in your data source!
